About Us

BookHub is one of the most unconventional bookstores out there. Our store believes that in order to enjoy a good book, you shouldn't look at the cover of the book. We stand by the quote "Don't judge a book by it's cover" because we want you to enjoy a book without worrying what it looks like. Go on a journey with one of our books, dive deep into the concepts of fantasy and fairytales.
